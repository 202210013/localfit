1. About This Guide

This manual is a detailed, customer-focused guide for using LocalFit from registration until post-order actions such as pickup confirmation and ratings.

2. System Overview

LocalFit is a web-based shopping and order tracking platform with size-based ordering, order status tracking, ratings, and realtime messaging.

3. Account Setup and Login

3.1 Register

  1. Open Register page.
  2. Enter required account details.
  3. Submit and proceed to Login.

3.2 Login

  1. Enter credentials.
  2. Sign in to access protected pages.

3.3 Logout

Always log out when using shared devices.

4. User Dashboard and Navigation

  • /product-main
  • /product-listing
  • /cart
  • /orders
  • /messages

5. Browsing and Searching Products

  1. Open Product Listing.
  2. Use search to find products quickly.
  3. Check product price, image, and available sizes.

6. Selecting Size, Quantity, and Pickup Date

  • Size selection is required before ordering.
  • Quantity must be valid.
  • Pickup date may be required depending on the order flow.

7. Cart Management

  1. Add item to cart after selecting size and quantity.
  2. Review cart details.
  3. Update quantity or remove items as needed.

8. Placing an Order

  1. Confirm cart content.
  2. Verify required fields.
  3. Submit order and wait for success message.

9. Order Tracking and Status Guide

  • Pending
  • Ready for Pickup
  • Declined
  • Completed

Use Orders page to monitor each status change.

10. Pickup Confirmation Process

  1. Open order in Ready for Pickup.
  2. Click Confirm Pickup.
  3. Enter OR number if prompted.
  4. Confirm completion.

11. Ratings and Reviews

  • Available only for completed orders.
  • Rating scale is 1 to 5 stars.
  • One rating per order per user.

12. Messaging and Chat

  1. Open Messages page.
  2. Type and send your concern.
  3. Watch conversation updates in realtime.

13. Notifications and Unread Indicators

Unread indicators are cleared by opening and reading the latest messages.

14. Common Errors and Troubleshooting

14.1 Cannot Log In

Check credentials, clear browser storage, and retry.

14.2 Cannot Add to Cart

Confirm size and quantity are valid, then retry.

14.3 Orders Not Showing

Refresh page and re-login if session expired.

14.4 Cannot Submit Rating

Ensure order is completed and not previously rated.

14.5 Messaging Delay

Refresh Messages page and check internet stability.

15. Security and Privacy Best Practices

  • Use strong passwords.
  • Do not share account credentials.
  • Log out from shared devices.
  • Avoid sending sensitive data in chat.

16. Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to order? Yes, ordering requires login.

Can I rate more than once? No, one rating per completed order.

Why is pickup confirmation blocked? The order may not be in Ready for Pickup status yet.

17. Glossary

  • Cart: temporary list before placing order.
  • Ready for Pickup: order is available for claiming.
  • Completed: final order status.
  • OR Number: reference number used during pickup confirmation when required.
